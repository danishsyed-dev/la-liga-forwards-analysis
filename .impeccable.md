## Design Context

### Users
- **Target Audience**: Football analysts, sports journalists, and dedicated football fans who appreciate detailed historical comparisons.
- **Context**: Accessing the app to study data-driven rankings of La Liga forwards, explore specific performance trends, or upload custom datasets for evaluation.
- **Job to Be Done**: To quickly compare legendary players across multiple metrics and draw clear, credible conclusions from verified data.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice/Tone**: Authoritative, analytical, editorial, and sophisticated.
- **3-Word Personality**: Editorial, Utilitarian, Precise.
- **Emotional Goals**: The interface should evoke credibility, intellectual curiosity, and aesthetic appreciation.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Athletic Editorial. A premium publication style (inspired by *The Athletic* and high-end sports infographics) featuring high typography contrast, strict container alignment, and intentional data-first spacing.
- **Color Theme**: Sophisticated sports-dark mode. Deep charcoal base with clean slate borders, accented by selective emerald green and gold indicators.
- **Typography Pairing**: Elegant display serif for headers (e.g., Playfair Display or Georgia) paired with a clean, highly readable geometric sans-serif for numbers and data tables (e.g., Satoshi, Outfit, or Inter-alternative styling).

### Design Principles
1. **Color is Information**: Never use colors for decoration. Accent colors (emerald/gold) represent primary actions or rank positions.
2. **Flatten the Hierarchy**: Avoid card-in-card nesting and generic boxes. Use subtle borders, layout asymmetry, and varied typography sizing to define boundaries.
3. **Purity of Numbers**: Render all numerical statistics with tabular numbers (`font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`) and clean alignments to make reading tables effortless.
4. **Deliberate Easing**: Micro-interactions must feel responsive and natural, using exponential deceleration without bounce.
